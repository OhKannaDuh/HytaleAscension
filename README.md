# Hytale Ascension

A Hytale RPG mod.

## Status

Hytale Ascension is a progression‑driven RPG overhaul designed to make every moment in Hytale feel meaningful. Instead of flat stats and static combat, Ascension turns your character into a growing, evolving hero - shaped by your choices, your party, and the challenges you take on.

Whether you’re exploring with friends, carving out your own build, or preparing for future raids and dungeons, Ascension aims to bring long‑term depth and a sense of adventure to every play session.

##  Features

- Leveling System - Earn experience by dealing damage and grow stronger as you play.

- Attribute Points - Each level grants points you can invest to shape your character’s build and playstyle.

- Party System - Team up with other players, share experience, and explore the world together.



## Planned Features

- Job/Class System - Distinct classes with unique abilities, progression paths, and skill unlocks.

- Party Radar Integration - See your party members’ positions directly on your radar for easier coordination.

- Support & Utility Abilities - Class‑specific skills that interact with party members, such as buffs, heals, or targeted effects.

- Class‑Themed Gear - Equipment tailored to different jobs and attribute spreads.

- Endgame Encounters - Ambitious long‑term content including raid bosses, challenging dungeons, and large‑scale battles.
